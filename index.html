What is Biblical Fellowship?

In the New Testament, the word "church" (ekklesia) means "the called-out ones" - not a building, but a community of believers gathered in fellowship. The early church met in homes, shared meals, studied Scripture together, and supported one another.

This website creates digital spaces for believers to experience the same authentic fellowship that characterized the early church - building relationships, growing in faith, and encouraging one another in our walk with Christ.

"And they devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and the fellowship, to the breaking of bread and the prayers."
- Acts 2:42
